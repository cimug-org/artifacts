UCAIug - Open Source Artifacts

Standard Series Description
IEC 61968 UCA TF14 open source artifacts that support standards developed within WG14 and published in the IEC 61968 series for interfaces supporting enterprise business functions for utility operations.
IEC 61970 UCA TF13 open source artifacts that support standards developed within WG13 and published in the IEC 61970 series of standards for software interfaces for operation and planning of the elecric grid.
IEC 62325 UCA TF16 open source artifacts that support standards developed within WG16 and published in the IEC 62325 series of standards related to energy market communications.
IEC 62746 UCA TF21 open source artifacts that support standards developed within WG21 and published in the IEC 62746 series of standards.
